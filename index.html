Yingbin Zhou   周英斌

I am currently a Ph.D student (from Sep. 2022) at the Future Network of Intelligence Institute (FNii) and the School of Science and Engineering (SSE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen), under the supervision of Prof. Shuguang Cui, and the co-supervision of Prof. Yaping Sun.

Prior to this, I received my M.S. and B.Eng. degrees from Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU) and Shantou University (STU) in 2022 and 2019, respectively.

My research is primarily centered around the utilization of Generative AI in Semantic Communication, Intelligent Networks and etc.
